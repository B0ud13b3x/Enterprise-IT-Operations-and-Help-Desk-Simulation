# Troubleshooting Journal

Real IT work is not just building things, it's fixing what breaks.
This journal documents technical problems I encountered (or deliberately
simulated) in the lab, using the **STAR method** used in professional
incident reporting and IT interviews.

**Format for every case:**
- **Situation** - What was the environment and what went wrong
- **Task** - What needed to be fixed
- **Action** - Exactly what I did to diagnose and resolve it
- **Result** - The outcome and what I learned
